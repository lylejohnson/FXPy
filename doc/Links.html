  • Obviously, FXPy wouldn't exist without FOX or Python. I do some work on the Windows port of FOX but its primary developer and architect is Jeroen van der Zijp.
  • I use Dave Beazley's SWIG to automatically generate most of FXPy.
  • A lot of things which would have been difficult to figure out have been previously figured out by Robin Dunn for wxPython. Studying his work in that project saved me a lot of time getting started with FXPy. Please note that I don't consider FXPy to be in "competition" with wxPython or any of the other GUI toolkits. I think each of them has its own merits and design philosophies and you should choose the one that works for you.
